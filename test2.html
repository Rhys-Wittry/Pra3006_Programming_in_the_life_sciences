Migration Viral Infections

This page aims to provide information on how viral infections are transmitted, what measures can be taken to prevent them, and how they affect public health. Viral infections are a global concern that can be prevented through good hygiene practices, vaccination, and an understanding of the transmission mechanisms. Here, we explore the most common ways of transmission, such as direct contact, respiratory droplets, and contaminated surfaces.
